Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2023

Introduction

Welcome to TinyPixels, your trusted online image compression and resizing service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

  • Images uploaded for compression or resizing
  • Usage data (time of upload, file sizes, compression rates)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address for security purposes
  • Optional email address if provided for notifications

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our image compression service
  • To improve our algorithms and service quality
  • To detect and prevent technical issues
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
  • To communicate service updates when necessary

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure HTTPS transmission
  • Encrypted image processing
  • Regular security audits
  • Automatic data deletion after processing

Image Storage Policy

Your images are:

  • Temporarily stored only during processing
  • Automatically deleted within 1 hour of processing
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Processed entirely on secure servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Request information about your data usage
