Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Introduction

At TinyPixels, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data:

  • All processing happens in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • No personal information is collected
  • Transparent and open-source operation

Data Protection

Your images are protected through:

  • Local browser-based processing only
  • No server uploads or storage
  • End-to-end privacy protection
  • Secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS)

Cookie Policy

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Theme preferences
  • Basic site functionality
  • Performance optimization
  • No tracking or marketing cookies

Analytics

Limited anonymous analytics include:

  • Aggregated usage statistics
  • No personal identifiers
  • Performance monitoring
  • Service improvement metrics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (if any)
  • Delete your data
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Contact us with concerns

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions:

  • Email: privacy@tinypixels.app
  • Response within 48 hours
  • Clear communication promise
  • Dedicated privacy team
