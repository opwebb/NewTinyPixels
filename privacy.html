Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Overview

TinyPixels is committed to protecting your privacy. Our service processes all images locally in your browser - your files never leave your device.

We collect minimal anonymous usage data to improve our service. We do not store or process your images on our servers.

We use local storage only to remember your preferences and settings.

We use essential cookies for basic site functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

We use basic analytics to understand usage patterns. All data is anonymized and aggregated.
