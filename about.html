About TinyPixels

Our Story

At TinyPixels, we believe in making image optimization accessible to everyone. Founded in 2023, our team is dedicated to providing free, privacy-focused image processing tools.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help users optimize their images without compromising on quality or privacy. We process everything locally in your browser, ensuring your data stays secure.
