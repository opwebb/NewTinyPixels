Our Story

Founded in 2023, TinyPixels emerged from a simple idea: everyone should have access to powerful image optimization tools without compromising privacy or quality.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to providing free, secure, and efficient image processing tools that respect your privacy. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your data never leaves your device.
